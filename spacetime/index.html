VCV Rack 2 instrument family

SpaceTime

One programmable field of stages. Up to eight independent ways through it.

Architecture

An instrument assembled to fit the patch.

SpaceTime deconstructs the programming surface and function generators of the Buchla 248 concept into a contiguous VCV family. Stage blocks own their data, heads share the field without sharing transport, and MIDI sits transparently between them.

Head ×1–8→MIDI→Program→Stage4 ×1–16

Module family

Six roles, one shared instrument.

Stage selection, modifiers, presets, scale, external inputs, and polyphonic output.

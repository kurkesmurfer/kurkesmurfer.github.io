10 HP function generator

Head

Each Head is an independent route through the stage field. Place one to eight directly left of MIDI or Program.

Transport and status

ControlAction
StartRun or resume. The input also supplies Sustain gate level and Enable threshold.
StopStop immediately.
AdvanceForce the next stage according to direction and region.
ResetReturn to the First stage of the active region.
DisplayLatch this head as Program's live edit display. Press again, or use Select, to release.

Green means running, yellow means holding or waiting for a selected clock source, and red means stopped or in Continuous addressing.

Addressing

ControlChoices
Address0–10 V position knob; scaling is selected on Program.
Int / ExtUse the knob or Address CV input.
ModeStrobe loads the addressed stage once; Sequential clocks through stages; Continuous sweeps the stage field directly from address.

Playback

ControlChoices
DirectionForward, Reverse, Pendulum, Random, Brownian
ClockInternal stage time, external CLK input, MIDI Clock, or Virtual Clock from MIDI CC
Div / Mult/16, /8, /4, /2, ×1, ×2, ×4, ×8, ×16
Time CVBipolar attenuverter for the Time CV input, scaling all stage intervals for this head.
LoopOne-shot, First–Last region, or full chain

Inputs

InputPurpose
StartStart pulse; Sustain gate; Enable >5 V threshold.
StopStop pulse.
AdvanceForce one stage transition.
StrobeLoad the stage selected by Address.
AddressExternal address voltage when Ext is selected.
CLKExternal clock edges.
TimePer-head modulation of interval duration.

Outputs

OutputSignal
CVRendered stage voltage; 1 V/oct when the stage is quantized.
TimeCurrent stage's time-slider value as CV.
RefDownward ramp spanning the interval.
AllPulse at every stage entry.
P1 / P2Programmed stage gates.
EOCEnd-of-cycle or stop event.

MIDI transport

The context menu identifies this head's fixed MIDI channel, shows its last CC, and enables Follow MIDI transport. When enabled, global MIDI Start, Stop, and Continue affect this head.

Glue Right

Glue Right terminates the left-hand fragment of a separated SpaceTime chain. Pair it with Glue Left on the same link number.

Placement

Place Glue Right immediately to the right of the fragment being continued elsewhere:

Head / Program / Stage4→Glue Right

A valid pair preserves normal stage and head numbering across the gap. It can split a chain between heads, between MIDI and Program, or between Stage4 blocks.

Link and readout

Select Link 1–8 from the context menu and choose the same number on the matching Glue Left. The readout shows the link and detected side.

LightMeaning
GreenPaired, unique, and compatible.
RedWaiting, mismatched, reversed, or duplicated.
OffNo valid SpaceTime neighbor.

Failure behavior

A duplicate endpoint or mismatched pair fails closed instead of connecting to an arbitrary fragment. The normal chain then becomes invalid or truncated, making the error visible on Program.

Scope

Glue is specific to SpaceTime and one Rack patch. It does not transport audio or CV and does not make generic VCV expanders wireless.
